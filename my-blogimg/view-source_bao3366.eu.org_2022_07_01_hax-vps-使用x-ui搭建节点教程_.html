<p><strong>Hax这个网站提供了免费的IPV6 VPS，以及免费的网络虚拟空间的服务。</strong></p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img decoding="async" width="1024" height="496" src="https://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/1.png" alt="" class="wp-image-15" srcset="http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/1.png 1024w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/1-300x145.png 300w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/1-768x372.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /></figure>



<h2 id="准备材料">准备材料:</h2>



<p>一枚TG账号</p>



<h2 id="注册VPS">注册VPS</h2>



<p>1.打开<a href="https://hax.co.id/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">官网</a>，点击右上角的“Register”注册</p>



<p>2.在Telegram上打开<a href="https://t.me/HaxTG_bot" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">@HaxTG_bot</a>，输入<code>/getid</code>命令获取自己的Telegram ID，复制粘贴到网页中，点击“Submit”按钮</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="1024" height="546" src="https://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/2.png" alt="" class="wp-image-16" srcset="http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/2.png 1024w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/2-300x160.png 300w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/2-768x410.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /></figure>



<p>3.稍后会在Telegram上收到bot发来的验证码，输入验证码，设置密码，登录账号</p>



<p>4.设置好VPS参数之后，点击“Create VPS”按钮</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="825" height="661" src="https://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/3.png" alt="" class="wp-image-17" srcset="http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/3.png 825w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/3-300x240.png 300w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/3-768x615.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 825px) 100vw, 825px" /></figure>



<p>5.等待1-3分钟，然后就出现了VPS的登录信息</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="824" height="661" src="https://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/4.png" alt="" class="wp-image-18" srcset="http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/4.png 824w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/4-300x241.png 300w, http://bao3366.eu.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/4-768x616.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 824px) 100vw, 824px" /></figure>

<p>如有IPV6</p>

<p>3.打开Xshell，新建一个会话</p>

<h1><strong>安装x-ui面板搭建节点方法</strong></h1>



<p>使用你获得的IPV4打开SSH，或者使用上面官方的SSH连接.</p>



<h3><strong>在VPS内安装WARP：</strong></h3>



<p>1.首先打开TUN模块（如果是KVM机器可跳过此步骤）。以Hax官网为例，打开官网的控制面板，点击“Enable TUN”按钮，稍等片刻后再点击“Restart”按钮重启VPS</p>



<p>vps开启tun<br>2.我们连接SSH，输入cat /dev/net/tun命令，检查TUN模块状态，如出现“cat: /dev/net/tun: File descriptor in bad state”时即为开启成功</p>



<p>3.复制以下安装命令</p>



<p>wget -N https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/fscarmen/warp/menu.sh &amp;&amp; bash menu.sh</p>



<p>4.选择语言，输入2选择简体中文</p>



<p>5.输入“1”安装WARP（IPV4连接走WARP的流量），当然你输入“2”也可以（全部连接走WARP的流量）</p>
